New Postdoctoral Position at ETH Zurich

I have started my new position as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Swiss Seismological Service (SED), ETH Zurich.

Read more

My research at ETH Zurich will focus on generative waveform modeling and earthquake dynamics modeling, with special emphasis on understanding the impact of fault geometry and material properties on rupture propagation and ground motion. I am excited to collaborate with the excellent team at the Swiss Seismological Service and contribute to advancing our understanding of seismic hazards.
