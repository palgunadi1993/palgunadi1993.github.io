Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in Geophysical Engineering at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) and a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Swiss Seismological Service (SED), ETH Zurich, Switzerland. My research focuses on Earthquake Dynamics and Deep Learning in Seismology and I'm the Principal Investigator of the Earthquake Mechanics Research Group at ITS.

I completed my PhD in Earth Science and Engineering at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Martin Mai. My research interests include earthquake rupture dynamics, induced seismicty, deep learning in seismology, and earthquake source mechanics.
